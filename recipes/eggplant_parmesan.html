Eggplant Parmesan

This simple eggplant Parmesan dish features stacked crispy, breaded eggplant slices that have been baked, not fried.
The blend of crunchy breadcrumbs, tomato sauce, mozzarella and Parmesan cheese will definitely be a hit!

Ingredients

  • Breadcrumbs
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan cheese
