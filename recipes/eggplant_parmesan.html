Eggplant Parmesan

This simple eggplant Parmesan dish features stacked crispy, breaded eggplant slices that have been baked, not fried.
The blend of crunchy breadcrumbs, tomato sauce, mozzarella and Parmesan cheese will definitely be a hit!

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggplant, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 4 cups Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 6 cups spaghetti sauce, divided
  • 1 (16 ounce) package mozzarella cheese, shredded and divided
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
