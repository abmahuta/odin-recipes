Pomelo Salad Rice

"An Asian-inspired salad that is both light
and refreshing, with only 4 SmartPoints per serving on the WW Green plan."

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces rice vermicelli
  • ½ large pomelo- peeled, membranes removed, and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • ¼ cup julienned carrot
  • ¼ cup julienned cucumber
  • 1 scallion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil; stir in rice vermicelli and return to a boil.
    Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, according to packaging instructions, 5 to 6 minutes, depending on thickness of noodles.
    Rinse with cold water and drain well.
  2. Transfer vermicelli to a bowl. Add pomelo, carrot, cucumber, scallion, and cilantro.
  3. Stir fish sauce, water, Thai sweet chili sauce, brown sugar,
    and rice vinegar together in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave for 20 seconds to dissolve the sugar.
    Whisk slightly and pour dressing over vermicelli mixture.
    Toss to combine. Serve immediately.
